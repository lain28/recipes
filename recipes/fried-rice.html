RECIPES

Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Coconut oil
  • Protein of choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Minced or powdered garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Basil
  • Pineapple (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook protein and eggs in coconut oil. Set aside.
  2. Fry the rice and add seasonings to taste.
  3. If including pineapple, then precook w/ proteins and eggs.
  4. Add back protein, eggs, and basil when rice is warmed through.
  5. Taste.
  6. If good it's done.
