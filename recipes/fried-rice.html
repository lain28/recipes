Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Protein of choice
  • Coconut oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Minced garlic or powdered
  • Basil
  • Oyster sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Eggs
  • Pineapple (optional)

Instructions

  1. Fry eggs in coconut oil with sprinkle of salt. Set aside.
  2. If applicable, stir fry your protein until cooked.
  3. Add day old or dried rice into pan, add seasonings (soy sauce, oyster sauce, garlic, etc.)
  4. Add back in the eggs, protein, and chopped basil.
