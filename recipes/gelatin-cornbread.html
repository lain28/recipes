RECIPES

Gelatin Cornbread

Ingredients

  • Masa flour ~120g (1 cup)
  • Coconut oil ~110g (0.5 cup)
  • Sugar ~100g (0.5 cup)
  • Egg 1
  • Non-fat milk powder ~60g (0.5 cup)
  • Gelatin (dissolved in water) ~30g
  • Baking soda ~2.5g (1/2 tsp)
  • Milk (as needed)

Instructions

  1. Melt coconut oil first.
  2. Mix all the ingredients, add milk until thick batter forms.
  3. Bake at 350F for 20-25 minutes if using cupcake pan, 35-40 minutes if using cake pan.
