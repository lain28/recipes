RECIPES

Chili Beef (Burmese)

Ingredients

  • Beef chunks or slices
  • Garlic (minced or powdered)
  • Ginger (minced or powdered)
  • Large onion
  • Basil
  • Jalapenos
  • Chiliflakes (as needed)
  • Cumin (A LOT)
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Oil (coconut & olive)
  • Bell peppers (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, salt, pepper, cumin, garlic, ginger, sugar, cornstarch, and olive oil (all eyeballed) for however long.
  2. Stir fry onion in coconut oil until browned. Then turn up the heat.
  3. Add in marinated beef and stir fry until browned.
  4. When browned, but not cooked all the way through, add sliced jalapenos (and bell peppers if applicable.)
  5. Mix and let simmer for 5-10 minutes. Towards end of the simmering cycle add in the basil.
  6. If the spice from jalapenos is not enough, add in chiliflakes.
  7. When it's reduced to a sauce, taste. If good then good. If needs more seasoning, add it and simmer another 5 min or so.
