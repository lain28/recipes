Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 375°F.
  2. Pat the chicken dry and coat lightly with oil.
  3. Season all sides evenly.
  4. Cook for 18 to 22 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  5. Check that the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
