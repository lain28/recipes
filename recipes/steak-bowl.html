RECIPES

Steak Bowl どん

Ingredients

  • Steak (e.g. ribeye, new york strip, whatever).
  • Large onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Rice wine or mirin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetables of choice (e.g. greens, mushrooms, whatever).
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Grill steaks like any other steak (w/ salt, pepper).
  2. Slice onions and brown in small pot w/ olive oil.
  3. When onions are soft, add equal parts soy sauce and water (~2 cups each).
  4. Add good amount of sugar to sauce (~0.33 cups). Taste.
  5. Add splash of mirin or rice wine, let simmer for ~5 min to cook out alcohol.
  6. Poach eggs (usually one egg per person).
  7. Cook vegetables any way you want.
  8. Assemble your bowl.
